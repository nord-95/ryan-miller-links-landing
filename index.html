Ryan Miller is an artist known for his unique and genre-defying sound. Pop, rock, dance, and a hint of electronic and experimental influences all come together in his music. Themes of love, sorrow, and the human experience are frequently explored in Miller's songs, which are both reflective and emotive. His melodies are intricate and catchy, and his lyrics are personal and relatable.
